# AZZRO - Requirement Freeze (Phase 0)

## 0.1 Business Scope
- **Domain**: Fashion eCommerce (Men / Women / Kids)
- **Model**: Multi-vendor marketplace, B2C
- **Geography**: India-first focus
  - GST Compliance
  - Cash on Delivery (COD)
  - Indian Logistics integration

## 0.2 Core Features (Benchmarks: Pantaloons, Myntra, Ajio)
- **Homepage**: Category-driven, visually rich.
- **Product Listing**: Rich filtering, sorting, infinite scroll/pagination.
- **Product Details**: High-quality imagery, varied variants, detailed descriptions.
- **Checkout**: Fast, streamlined, guest checkout options.
- **Search**: Robust, likely with suggestions/auto-complete.
- **CMS**: Dynamic control over banners and collections.
